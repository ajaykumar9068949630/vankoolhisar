ABOUT US

Thanks to our valued end users, trade partners and work force, we can proudly introduce ourselves as an established brand synonym to quality and trust. Having started in year 2002 as grill manufacturer in Hisar, Haryana to today delivering more than 20000 pcs a season in more than 8 states, we have travelled a long journey. This valuable experience of years, a motivated workforce, customer oriented top management and hard working dealers are what define us. Vankool today reaches to end users of 8 states through dedicated team of 13 distributors and more than 50 dealers. We have always kept our motto as “Customer First” and have always emphasized on delivering the best of quality at most affordable price. To our dealers and distributors, we have been a company that protect their interests and help them sail through rough and tough times. We are committed to deliver the best of the product and strengthen our services in all coming times.
